Laptop On Kaise Kare – Complete Beginner Guide

Agar aap beginner hain aur jaana chahte hain ki laptop on kaise kare, to yeh guide aapke liye hai.

Step 1: Charger Connect Karein

Ensure karein ki laptop ki battery charged ho. Agar battery low hai to charger connect karein.

Step 2: Power Button Dhundhein

  • Power button usually keyboard ke upar hota hai.
  • Kabhi kabhi side me bhi ho sakta hai.

Step 3: Power Button Press Karein

Power button ko ek baar press karein. Screen light up ho jayegi aur boot process start ho jayega.

Laptop On Nahi Ho Raha?

  • Check karein charger working hai ya nahi.
  • Battery remove karke dubara lagayein (agar removable ho).
  • Power button 10 second hold karein reset ke liye.

Boot Time Kitna Hota Hai?

Normal laptop 10–40 seconds me on ho jata hai depending on hardware and SSD/HDD.

Conclusion: Laptop on karna simple hai, bas power button press karna hota hai aur battery charged honi chahiye.
